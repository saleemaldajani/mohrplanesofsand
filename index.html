Parameters

For dry, cohesionless sand the angle of repose equals the friction angle, α ≈ φ. Inside the pile, slip happens on planes tilted θf = 45° + φ/2 from the major principal stress — which is why the Mohr circle’s tangent point sits at 2θf.
